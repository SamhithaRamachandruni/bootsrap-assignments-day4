Tips to Click Stunning Photos

  • Lighting Matters: The best natural lighting is during golden hours (early morning and late afternoon).
  • Rule of Thirds: Divide your frame into 9 sections and place your subject at intersections for better composition.
  • Use Leading Lines: Roads, fences, or rivers can guide the viewer’s eyes to the subject.
  • Adjust Focus and Depth: Use a wide aperture for portraits (blurred background) and a narrow aperture for landscapes.
  • Experiment with Angles: Try different angles, like low-angle or bird’s-eye view, to create unique perspectives.
  • Edit Smartly: Enhance contrast, sharpness, and brightness without over-processing.
